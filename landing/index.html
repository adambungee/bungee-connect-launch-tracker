The Solution

Everything You Need to Quote Like a Pro

Bungee Connect was built by carriers, for carriers. These features are ready to use right now — no credit card, no trial, no catch.

1

Multi-Stop Route Builder

Drag-and-drop reordering, auto-distance via OSRM, and interactive Leaflet/OpenStreetMap visualization. See your route come alive.

2

AI-Powered Chatbot

Type "Toronto to Hamilton to London" and the route auto-builds with costs calculated. Handles spelling mistakes and conversational input.

3

Cost Profile Wizard

Set up Dry Van, Reefer, or Flatbed profiles covering truck costs, insurance, fuel, wages, and dock/detention rates. Fully customizable.

4

Yard-Based Routing

Set your base yard and auto-calculate deadhead from yard to first pickup and back — a critical cost most tools ignore.

5

3-Tier Pricing Advice

Instantly see quotes at 20%, 30%, and 40% margin with a quality label: Low, Fair, Good, Great, or Outstanding.

6

Team Management

Admin vs. Sales roles with PIN-based access. Sales sees pricing advice only — no internal cost data exposed.

7

Quote History

Save and review every quote with full route and pricing details. Never lose a quote again.

8

Local P&D Detection

Auto-detects local routes under 100 km and factors in city traffic delays and waiting time for accurate local delivery pricing.
